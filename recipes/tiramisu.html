Ingredients:

  • Fresh cream
  • Cocoa powder
  • White flower
  • White sugar
  • Egg whites

Directions:

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl
  2. add egg whites and mix
  3. add fresh cream and mix
  4. put in a dish and refrigerate for 3 hours
  5. Once the mix has set, sprinkle with cocoa powder and serve
